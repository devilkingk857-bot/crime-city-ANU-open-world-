# 🏙️ ANU CITY 3D — Open World Survival Protocol

**ANU CITY 3D** is a high-performance, open-world action-survival game optimized for both PC and Mobile. Experience a gritty, atmospheric metropolis filled with danger, dynamic weather, and an elite arsenal of weapons.

![Game Logo](./game_logo_anu_city.png)

## 🚀 Key Features

### 🌌 Dynamic Atmosphere
- **Day/Night Cycle**: The city's lighting, fog, and sun dynamically transition from bright day to deep moonlit night.
- **Weather System**: Cinematic rain effects with "wet" road reflections and adaptive mood lighting.

### 🚁 Police Helicopter AI
- Reach 4 stars to trigger an **Aerial Pursuit**.
- The Police Helicopter orbits you and attacks with a high-speed minigun burst. 
- Use the **Auto-Aim** helper (on mobile) or your sniper/rocket skills to bring it down!

### ⚔️ Elite Arsenal
- **Katana**: Traditional melee sword for high-impact sweep attacks.
- **Flamethrower**: Cone-based fire damage to clear entire crowds of zombies.
- **Minigun**: Extreme fire rate for ultimate destruction (with a realistic movement speed penalty).
- **Classic Firearms**: Auto Rifle, Shotgun, Sniper, and RPG-7.

### 🏎️ Vehicle Variety
- **Sports (Veneno)**: High speed and sleek handling.
- **Heavy (Truck)**: Massive durability—unstoppable on the road.
- **Police Interceptor**: Balanced speed and durability for the law.
- **Physics**: Real water-resistance logic (slowdown when driving into the waterfront).

### ⚡ Optimized for Mobile
- **Custom Dual Joysticks**: Precise movement and aiming controls.
- **Proximity Culling**: Smart engine logic sleeps distant entities to save battery and boost FPS.
- **Instanced Foliage**: Massive 3D nature rendering with 99% reduced draw calls.

## 🕹️ Controls

### PC (Keyboard/Mouse)
- **WASD**: Movement
- **Mouse Move**: Aim
- **Left Click**: Fire / Attack
- **Shift**: Sprint
- **Space**: Jump
- **TAB/F**: Cycle Weapons
- **R**: Reload
- **V**: Toggle Camera View (Standard / Tactical)
- **G**: Throw Grenade
- **E / ENTER**: Enter/Exit Vehicle

### Mobile (Touch)
- **Left Joystick**: Move
- **Right Joystick**: Aim & Auto-Shoot
- **HUD Buttons**: Jump, Sprint, Reload, Switch, Drive, Camera.

## 🛠️ Tech Stack
- **Engine**: Three.js (WebGL 3D)
- **Language**: JavaScript / HTML5 / CSS3
- **Optimized**: Custom BufferGeometry and Instanced Rendering for 60FPS performance on mobile devices.
